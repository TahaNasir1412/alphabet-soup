STEP 1
Type keyword
Enter the main word/phrase you want to research. Keep it simple.

Select where your users are and what language they type in. Brazil = Brazil + Portuguese.
STEP 3

AI analyses niche and builds modifier lists. Check the ✅ AI indicator — green means AI is working.

Filter & export
