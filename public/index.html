📖 How to use this tool
STEP 1
Type your keyword
Enter the main word or phrase you want to research in the box on the left. Keep it simple — just the keyword.
STEP 2
Pick your country & language
Select the country your users are in, and the language they type in. If your site is for Brazil, pick Brazil + Portuguese.
STEP 3
Choose sweeps & click Generate
Keep all sweeps ON for the most keywords. Hit Generate. The AI figures out your niche automatically and starts collecting suggestions.
STEP 4
Review & export
Keywords appear live as they're found. Filter by sweep type, click any keyword to copy it, or export everything as a CSV file.
BONUS
Answer AI questions
If the AI asks you a clarifying question, click an answer. This makes the modifiers even more accurate for your specific niche.
THEN
Get search volume
Export the CSV → paste into Ahrefs or Google Keyword Planner to see which keywords get the most searches.
